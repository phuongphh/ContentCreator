# [Process] Content Creator must submit video script as review article

> GitHub: [#37](https://github.com/phuongphh/ContentCreator/issues/37) • Phase: unknown

## Summary
Content creators must submit the **actual video script** (the article used to generate the video) for review — not a separate summary or rewritten version.

## Problem
Currently, the article submitted for review may differ from the script used to produce the video. This causes a mismatch between written content and published video, making review ineffective.

## Requirement
- **Long video day** → Submit the full-length script/article (as used in video production)
- **Short video day** → Submit the short-form script/article (as used in video production)
- The submitted article **must be identical** to the content narrated/presented in the video

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Content creator submits the video script as the review artifact — not a separate article
- [ ] Article length matches the video format for that day (long/short)
- [ ] Reviewer can cross-check article against video and they should match 100%
- [ ] No separate summary articles — one source of truth per video

## Notes
- This applies to all video formats: long-form, short-form, reels, etc.
- The review step happens **before** video is published
